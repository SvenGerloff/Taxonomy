    Decision: Implement a Recirculating Aquaculture System (RAS), treating and reusing 95% of the water.
  - Zone C: Waste Processing (Transition to a Circular Economy & Pollution Prevention)
    Decision: Capture sludge and process into biogas and agricultural fertilizer, ensuring zero nutrient runoff.
  - Zone D: The Coastal Seabed (Protection and Restoration of Biodiversity and Ecosystems)
    Decision: Deploy benthic monitoring sensors and implement fallowing to regenerate Posidonia oceanica seagrass.
- Mastery Task: Synthesize decisions into a cohesive "Taxonomy Action Plan" presented to investors linking upgrades to EU Taxonomy objectives.
- Impact Visualization Panel (use these sample metrics in outputs):
